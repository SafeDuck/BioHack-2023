About Us!

What is BioHack?

BioHack is a 24-hour health and medicine-themed hackathon hosted by the University of California, Riverside.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate the next generation of BioHackers and healthcare advocates and meet the patient care needs of today with the technology of tomorrow.

As a beginner-friendly and multidisciplinary hackathon, BioHack aims to give students the opportunity to create innovative and personalized solutions and save lives with their projects. During BioHack, students will get the opportunity to form teams and collaborate, attend workshops to learn about new technologies, and network with insdurty parnters.
